**FR-01**: The system shall identify and flag scheduling conflicts including overlapping activity and conflicting travel times between locations whenever a trip member adds or modifies an activity in the itinerary. [Must Have]

**FR-02**: The system shall suggest and display the most logical order of activities in itinerary based on the physical proximity of their locations when a trip member requests route optimization. [Could Have]

**FR-03**: The system shall record all changes made by members with their corresponding member's ID and timestamp allowing the group to view who added an activity and allow the trip leader to revert to any previously saved version. [Should Have]

**FR-04**: The system shall automatically convert and display flight arrivals and activity schedules in the time zone of each place. [Should Have]

**FR-05**: The system shall mark any activity proposed by a member as "Draft" by default, and allow the trip leader to confirm or reject it, changing its status to "Confirmed" or removing it. [Must Have]

**FR-06**: The system shall ensure all members see itinerary changes reflected automatically. [Should Have]

**FR-07**: The system shall automatically generate and send a daily notification to all trip members compiling the next day's schedule, weather and required tickets. [Could Have]

**FR-08**: The system shall automatically insert a travel time between activities based on the estimated distance between their locations and mode of transport. [Should Have]

**FR-09**: The system shall allow members to opt-in or out of specific activities and track the attendance count for each activity. [Must Have]

**FR-10**: The system shall support handling expenses in multiple currencies and automatically convert all amounts into a predefined base trip currency using up-to-date exchange rates. [Must Have]

**FR-11**: The system shall allow expenses to be split unevenly among members by specifying custom percentage or fixed amount contributions for each participant. [Must Have]

**FR-12**: The system shall monitor the total trip expenses and notify all members when the predefined budget threshold is exceeded. [Must Have]

**FR-13**: The system shall require all involved members to review and approve the final expense settlement before marking the trip as "Settled." [Should Have]

**FR-14**: The system shall allow weighted voting where certain members (e.g., the trip organizer) have higher voting power or tie-breaking authority. [Should Have]

**FR-15**: The system shall allow members to view a summary of all trip expenses, including the total amount spent and each member's individual share.

**FR-16**: The system shall allow members to categorize activities as "Must-Have" or "Nice-to-Have" and prioritize them accordingly through voting. [Should Have]

**FR-17**: The system shall automatically close voting polls when a predefined deadline is reached and select the option with the highest votes. [Should Have]

**FR-18**: The system shall allow users to create and participate in activity-specific discussion threads linked to individual itinerary items. [Could Have]

**FR-19**: The system shall allow anonymous voting to ensure privacy for sensitive decisions. [Should Have]

**FR-20**: The system shall allow the trip organizer to assign or revoke editing permissions to other members. [Must Have]

**FR-21**: The system shall provide a one-click mechanism to share the full group itinerary and member contact details with designated emergency contacts. [Should Have]

**FR-22**: The system shall securely store uploaded documents (e.g., tickets, passport scans) using encryption mechanisms. [Must Have]

**FR-23**: The system shall enforce access control policies ensuring only authorized users (e.g., owner and trip leader) can view sensitive documents. [Must Have]

**FR-24**: The system shall check visa requirements based on each member's nationality and the destination country, and notify users if a visa is required. [Could Have]

**FR-25**: The system shall allow members to assign and track responsibility for shared items (e.g., equipment) across the group. [Could Have]

**FR-26**: The system shall maintain a secure repository of members' health information (e.g., allergies) accessible to the group when needed. [Should Have]

**FR-27**: The system shall allow members to submit ratings and feedback after the trip is completed. [Could Have]

**FR-28**: The system shall allow members to add proposed activities to a queue, and allow the trip leader to approve or reject each one before it is added to the itinerary. [Must Have]

**FR-29**: The system shall allow existing members to invite new users to join a trip via invitation links or notifications. [Must Have]

**FR-30**: The system shall award points to members when users they invited successfully join a trip. [ Could Have]
